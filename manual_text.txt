COMPANY OVERVIEW 

Q: What is Tru-Marine? 
Headquartered in Singapore and established in 1977, Tru-Marine brings 49 years of experience, earning the trust of leading shipping companies worldwide. With a global network of 8 strategically located service stations across Asia, Europe, and the Middle East, we deliver integrated MRO solutions for turbochargers, governors, fuel systems, and engines, supporting critical operations across marine and industrial sectors.

Q: What does a turbocharger do? 
A turbocharger increases engine efficiency by compressing air into the combustion chamber, allowing more fuel to be burned and producing more power. 

Shape 

Q: What are signs of turbocharger problems? 

Loss of power  

Increased fuel consumption  

Excessive smoke  

Unusual noise or vibration  

Oil leakage  

Shape 

Q: When should a turbocharger be serviced? 

Based on running hours  

During scheduled maintenance  

When abnormal performance is observed
